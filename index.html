Remember that Objectives define what is to be achieved. They are significant, concrete, action-oriented and inspirational. Each objective is measured by 3-5 Key Results (KRs). These KRs benchmark how we get to the objective. They are specific, time-bound, aggressive yet realistic, measurable and verifiable.
